Weight & Metabolic Health

Mental health and metabolism—treated together.

Struggling with weight is often not just about willpower. We address the metabolic and mental health factors that impact weight, energy, and overall wellness.

Services include:

  • GLP-1 evaluation and management
  • Peptide therapy
  • NAD+ support
  • Personalized assessment
  • Emotional eating & stress-related weight gain

Ideal for patients who:

  • Feel stuck despite dieting or exercise
  • Experience weight gain with anxiety, depression, or medications
  • Want medically guided, sustainable results

Root-Cause Focus

At Be Well Collaborative, we don't just manage symptoms—we work to understand why they're happening.

Mental health is deeply connected to:

  • Metabolism and blood sugar balance
  • Genetics, hormones, and inflammation
  • Sleep and sleep patterns
  • Nutrition and lifestyle

Our approach integrates these factors into a comprehensive, personalized plan—so you can experience meaningful, lasting improvement.
